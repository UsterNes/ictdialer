Send a Single Transmission ICTCore allows to support different kind of transmissions like Voice Call, SMS, Email and Fax to send message to a single contact.

Voice Call This transmission dials number from a contact list and on answer plays a recorded message. Send Voice Call
SMS Transmission This transmission selects number from a contact list and simply broadcast a text message to the contact group. Send SMS
Email Transmission This transmission sends email to the specified email address of your contact. Send Email
Fax Transmission This transmission dials number from a contact list and on answer transfer fax documents. Send Fax
IVR Transmission This transmission dials number from a contact list and on answer plays the IVR menu. Send IVR
Launch a Campaign ICTCore supports complete campaign management. Each user can manage, monitor, and run multiple campaigns separately and independently to send message to contact group.

Voice Campaign This campaign dials number from a contact list and on answer plays a recorded message. Send Voice Call
SMS Campaign This campaign selects number from a contact list and simply broadcast a text message to the contact. Send SMS
Email Campaign This campaign sends email to the specified email address of your contact group. Send Email
Fax Campaign This campaign dials number from a contact list and on answer transfer fax documents. Send Fax
IVR Campaign This campaign dials number from a contact list and on answer play the IVR menu. Send IVR
